Our Longueuil web design pricing

When shopping for a web design in Longueuil, you need to be prepared to spend at least $2000. This is the bare minimum to be expected for a high-quality website in Longueuil. A website is an investment in the long term, much like a house. Please be realistic with your budget and be sure to account for essential website fees such as: hosting, domain, maintenance and extra features. The more features and pages a website will have, the higher the cost will be.

In average, it takes between 3 to 6 weeks to build a website. You can think of each website as a car with a specific make, model and year: not all cars cost the same, nor do they all share the same build. In the end, every website is unique and therefore the production time and price can vary considerably.
